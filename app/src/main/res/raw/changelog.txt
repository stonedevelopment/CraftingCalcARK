    $ Always Show
      % How to use this app
      _ Increasing quantity
      * Single click any Engram thumbnail to increase quantity by 1.
      * Long click an Engram thumbnail from the main list (above) to open a detailed view of Engram, then adjust the dial to quantity and press Save button.
      _ Decreasing quantity
      * Long click an Engram thumbnail in the Crafting Queue (below) to decrease quantity by 1.
      * Long click an Engram thumbnail from the main list (above) to open a detailed view of Engram, then either adjust the dial down or simply click Remove button.
